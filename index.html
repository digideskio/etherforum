What is Etherforum?

Etherforum is an experimental, proof-of-concept, distributed forum built on top of Ethereum. This means all the posts are cryptographically signed and stored in the blockchain.

Why?

A forum is a starting place for discussions, ideas & knowledge sharing in general, providing a distributed architecture may guarantee the permanence of all the interesting contents in it.
